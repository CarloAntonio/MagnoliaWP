Mission

Who We Are

A residential care facility for elderly individuals with a hospice and dementia waiver located in beautiful Concord, California.

One of the few State and Federal approved assisted living waiver provider in Concord, California that helps support low income elderly.

We offer 24/7 non-medical care and support to our elderly clients at our facility.

All services are provided by experienced and well-trained staff, license and insured caregivers.

  • Personal care and assistance with daily activities
  • Meal preparation
  • Housekeeping
  • Laundry
  • Help with errands
  • Assistance to medical/dental appointments
  • Respite care
  • Custom created care
  • 24 hour admission
  • Group activities and trips
  • Hospice Care Waiver

Accommodation

  • Resident(s) ages from 60 years and older may be non-ambulatory
  • Resident(s) that needs 24 hours housing with supervision and assistance with ADL care
  • Resident(s) that needs supervision and assistance with medication preparation
  • Resident(s) that needs supporting care and supervision receiving home health nurse
  • Resident(s) with Dementia and Memory Problems
  • Resident(s) with restricted allowable health condition provided, the resident(s) can care for themselves and/or by their skilled professional

Why A Care Home?

  • 24-hour attention by reliable and compassionate caregivers
  • Smallest client needs and requests are responded quickly and necessary, health changes are reported to the client’s primary care doctor and responsible party
  • Independence and privacy
  • Ability for client’s to interact with family and friends freely
  • Ideal for clients who are at risk of falling
  • Caregivers can identify “red flags” which may indicate a client’s need for immediate medical attention or a safety issue
  • Cost effective
  • With proper prearranged authority, family can monitor the care of client through the caregiver’s daily log notes or progress notes
